How It Works

Three steps, one result

LEPS checks whether you are likely to qualify for a loan based on your financial details. No paperwork needed or office visit. You fill in a form, the system runs the numbers, and you get your result on the spot.

  1. 01

    Fill in your details

    You will be asked for basic personal information, your monthly income, your BVN and NIN, and the loan amount you have in mind. The form is split into four short sections so it does not feel overwhelming.

  2. 02

    The system assesses your application

    Once you submit, a machine learning model trained on thousands of loan records evaluates your information. It looks at your income, identity verification, loan history, and the amount you are requesting, then produces a score between 0 and 100.

  3. 03

    You get your result immediately

    Your score, eligibility decision (Eligible, Not Eligible, or Under Review), and an estimated loan amount appear on screen straight away. A copy of the result is also saved to your account so you can review it later.

This is an academic demonstration. Results are produced by a trained model and are not a formal financial decision from any lending institution.
